The Science of Blockchain Conference (SBC) 2025

August 3-7, 2025
Martin Luther King Jr. Building, UC Berkeley
David Brower Center, Berkeley

The conference is co-organized by IC3, the Stanford Center for Blockchain Research (CBR), and UC Berkeley RDI.

The conference focuses on technical innovations in the blockchain ecosystem, and brings together researchers and practitioners working in the space. We aim to foster collaboration among the different communities working on blockchain protocols, cryptography, distributed systems, secure computing, and crypto-economics.
